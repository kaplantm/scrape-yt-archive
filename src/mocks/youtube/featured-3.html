The Tenth of my Re-enactment series.



Two weeks have gone by since my last video was uploaded... I've been lazy, I admit it.



This is one of my favorite scenes of all time. John Candy's character goes to his niece's school to talk to her assistant principal about her behavior. He rips her a new one.
